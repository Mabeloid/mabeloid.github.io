ninjigalaxy sent me an explanation of how picross boards are stored in mario's picross' rom and commissioned me to make a tool to inject custom ones

after i made that i wanted to figure out how to replace the text and i've been moderately successful at it

the download contains three scripts:

  • picross_board_main.py replaces a board in the ROM with a new board from a text file or small image
  • picross_dump_text_main.py dumps dialogue and puzzle names to editable text files
  • picross_replace_text_main.py lets you replace dialogue or puzzle names with a file dumped by the abovementioned script
